About Me

A bit about who I am, what drives me, and where I'm headed.

I'm Michael, a first-year Computer Engineering student at Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario. I'm drawn to the lowest layers of the stack — kernels, schedulers, memory management, and everything that makes software actually run on hardware.

My goal is to become an operating systems engineer, building the foundational software that everything else depends on. I'm especially interested in Linux internals, real-time systems, and performance-critical code.
